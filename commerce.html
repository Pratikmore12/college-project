COMMERCE FACULTY

Today we are in the midst of international trade and business, and individuals wishing to make a career in
the field need to get a degree or post-grad degree in commerce. The department of commerce understands
the needs and has designed its courses to equip students with the necessary knowledge and skills needed in
their choice of career. The course is conducted in English and the teaching staff on the overall development of the student.
The library is stocked with books and reference material that help and guide students through their academic
years at the institute.
