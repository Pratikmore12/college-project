YCMOU FACULTY

  • T.C.College has started Courses Under YCMOU(Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University)
  • To Take admission students can contact YCMOU Office.
  • Student should take admission only through the YCMOU Office.
  • Student should pass Preliminary Examination before taking admission to F.Y.B.A. , F.Y.B.Com
  • Student passed Preliminary Exam and F.Y.B.A. / F.Y.B.Com is equivalent to 10th /12th student.
  • Degree taken through YCMOU is equivalent to degree taken from any other university.
